# About Bookshelf

Bookshelf is a reading discovery and personal library companion app built by **Huda AbuSharr**.

## What Bookshelf Offers

- Discover books by topic and category
- Search and browse book metadata
- Save, favorite, and mark books as read
- Sync your reading state with your account
- Scan book covers with camera or gallery to identify books quickly
- Get optional AI-powered summaries and recommendations
- Use the Movie-to-Book feature to find books with similar themes
- Optional premium purchase to remove ads

## Transparency

Bookshelf provides dedicated pages for:

- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Use
- Data Deletion Policy
- Support
